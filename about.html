About the author

Nick Proctor

I'm Nick Proctor, a Junior studying Games and Interactive Media at Michigan State University. I am also minoring in Game Design and Development. I am from Canton, Michigan, where I've lived most of my life. I have three siblings - two younger brothers and an older sister, as well as two dogs and two cats.

On this blog, I like writing about music and my opinions related to it. I listen to a wide range of music including but not limited to: rock, alternative, pop-punk, pop, indie, folk, emo, hyperpop, rap, EDM, and more. My favorite bands/artists include: blink-182, Paramore, Alex G, Wolf Alice, The Beatles, Radiohead, Motion City Soundtrack, The Smashing Pumpkins, Weezer, Tally Hall, My Chemical Romance, Animal Collective, and many more!
